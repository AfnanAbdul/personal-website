Adwait Jog | Spring 2025

Comprehensive exploration of the micro-architectural and architectural aspects of general-purpose graphics processing units (GPUs). It covered a range of top-tier architecture conference and journal papers to address key research challenges related to GPU architectures.

Bayesian Machine Learning

Teague Henry | Fall 2024

Bayesian approaches explicitly account for the uncertainty present in most machine learning problems. This uncertainty derives from both randomness in observational processes and incompleteness in problem understanding. This course focused on building models from data that provide predictions and quantify the uncertainty in these predictions.

Statistical Learning

Michael Porter | Fall 2024

Fundamentals of data mining and machine learning within a common statistical framework. Topics include regression, classification, clustering, resampling, regularization, tree-based methods, ensembles, boosting, and algorithmic bias. Coursework is conducted in the R programming language.
